A Day in Rome 🏛
Colosseum · Gladiator School · Trastevere · La Città Eterna
Hour by Hour — June 13
7:30 AM · Morning ☕
Espresso at a local bar
Stand at the counter like a Roman — €1.10. Cornetto. Find a bar where locals outnumber tourists.
8:30 AM · 🔥 Colosseum Arena Floor
Enter through the Gladiator's Gate
Stand where gladiators fought. Kids under 18 FREE — bring ID. Ticket includes Roman Forum + Palatine Hill.
10:30 AM · Roman Forum + Palatine
Walk where Caesar walked
Included in ticket. Sacred Way, Arch of Titus, Temple of Saturn. Preteens find this gripping.
12:30 PM · Testaccio Market 🍕
Where actual Romans eat
Supplì €2, pizza al taglio, porchetta. Cheap, authentic, extraordinary.
2:00 PM · ⚔️ GLADIATOR SCHOOL
2-hour training · Appian Way
Taxi ~€20 from Forum. Tunic, swords, certificate. Kids beg to return the next day.
⏰ Booked on GetYourGuide 1–2 weeks ahead. Free cancel 24 hrs.
5:00 PM · Gelato + Golden Hour 🍦
Giolitti — since 1900
Best in Rome. Sit on a fountain. Watch the city breathe. Then walk to Trastevere at sunset.
7:30 PM · Aperitivo → Dinner 🍷
Freni e Frizioni → Da Enzo al 29
€10 Spritz + free buffet. Then Da Enzo for carbonara and cacio e pepe.
10 PM · Trevi Fountain
Half the crowd, twice the magic
After 9 PM only. Toss a coin. Come back someday.

🛶 Venice Days
Gondola · Doge's Palace · Burano · Cicchetti · Disembark
Two Days in Venice
Day 1 Morning
Doge's Palace + Bridge of Sighs
Book ahead. Secret prisons, hidden passages — preteens completely absorbed.
Day 1 Afternoon
Private gondola · back canals
Skip Grand Canal — back-canal route through Cannaregio. Silent, narrow, impossibly romantic. €80–100/boat (5 people).
Day 1 Evening
Cicchetti bar crawl · Cannaregio
Best €15 dinner in Venice. House wine + crostini. 500 years unchanged.
Day 2 Morning
Murano + Burano island ferry
Glass-blowing on Murano (free), then Burano — coloured houses, most photogenic place in Italy.
Day 2 · Disembark June 22
Train Venice → Milan → Como ~3.5 hrs

June 23–24, 2026 · After the Cruise
🌊 Lake Como
Bellagio · Varenna · Villa Balbianello · Lake ferry
Two Days on the Lake
June 23 · Arrive
Train Venice → Milan → Como (~3.5 hrs)
Stay in Varenna — quieter, same views, more charm. 10-min ferry to Bellagio.
June 23 Afternoon
Villa Balbianello 🌿
Boat taxi from Lenno. Terraced gardens over the lake. Casino Royale + Star Wars filmed here.
June 24 · Full day at leisure
Ferry-hop the villages
Day pass ~€20. Bellagio, Varenna, Menaggio, Tremezzo. Swim off the docks.
June 24 Evening
Last Italian dinner on the water
Risotto al pesce persico (lake perch). The last supper in Italy. Do it right.
🌿 Tip: sunrise from Varenna's dock changes how you think about mornings. Set an alarm.
